Psychic Letter Game


HOW TO PLAY:

Guess the letter the computer is thinking of to prove you are psychic.

Use the letters on your keyboard to guess.

You are only allowed the pick letters (for your benefit).

Once you pick a letter you are not allowed to guess it again (also for your benefit).

You have 10 guesses to guess the correct letter.

If you succeed, a win will be added to the tally.

If you fail, a loss will be added to the tally.
